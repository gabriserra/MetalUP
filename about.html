Metal UP it's a new arcade game based on 90's SNK Metal Slug. Purpose of game it's to score more points as you can do! Marco is the main character but in this mission he doesn't have his gun! YOU have to help him, drawing a platform where he can jump. Obviously there are rebel soldier! Keep attention!

How To Play:

Click on Play Button visible on each page and wait entire game loading (it would require a minute for low bandwidth connection). When game it's ready click on screen to begin! Click and keep pressed left mouse button for draw a platform. To finish drawing leave mouse button! If player die, you can submit score (ONLY IF YOU ARE LOGGED!) and click on restart button to play again.

Features:
  • Less is the lenght of platform, more is wide Marco's jump!
  • Rebel soldier kill you only on a collision
  • Game border permit you to bounce back
  • Platform became concrete only when mouse button is released
  • Game is sensible to touch screen display
  • You can submit score and check the rank after a registration
